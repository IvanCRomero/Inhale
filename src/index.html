Inhale quality, inhale health, breath with us

What is inhale?

Inhale is a project aimed at collecting and analysing data in favour of combating air pollution and our environment in general. It is undoubtedly a necessity that we have today to take these records to transform them into media solutions, besides that it is not only for the individual good, but as a collective, as a species and responsible for our home, the earth.

Why inhale?

Inhale has established itself as a company to manage and lead a change in the alarming levels of CO2 in our atmosphere. And it is already alarming, the tons of this pollutant in our ecosystem entering as a trigger for climate change and respiratory diseases.

We are a solution for the future and the well-being of our species and terrestrial biodiversity, join us on this journey to change the air we breathe.

Breath

How will inhale act?

Inhale is developing a system to collect co2 levels through a sensor sensitive to these airborne particles. The fist step will be its inclusions in educational establishments, both primary and secondary schools. Following a trace also for small companies regulating also the health of students, teachers, workers etc. This information will be stored but also analysed and notified to their contractors, in order to propose solutions together.
